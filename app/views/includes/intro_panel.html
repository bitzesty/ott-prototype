Are you trading with Northern Ireland? From January 1st 2021, Northern Ireland is operating under a dual tariff regime and the rules may differ if you are importing goods that are then at risk of being transported to the Republic of Ireland. In that case, use the Northern Ireland (EU) Tariff.
